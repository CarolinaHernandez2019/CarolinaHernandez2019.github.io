About me

Jenny Carolina Hernández Cardozo

Bogotá, Colombia

I have 8+ years of experience in social data analysis with Colombian public institutions and organizations like the IDB. My focus: monitoring systems, impact evaluation in education and youth, and turning household survey data into information for public policy. I studied economics and demography, but much of what I know comes from continuous self-learning.
